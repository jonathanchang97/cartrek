    Privacy Policy

    Information we Collect

    By logging on to this website through Facebook you give us access to your name, Facebook profile picture, and email address. We also collect all information you enter into your CarTrek public profile, and we keep track of all treks you take part in as a driver or a passenger. This website uses cookies.

    What We Do with This Information

    Excluding any information Facebook may collect from your account and activities, we will not share your information with any third-parties. Your contact information will not be publicly available, and will only be shared with other users who are passengers of yours or are driving you. We may use information about your treks to improve our service and yours and other users' experiences. CarTrek may use the information you provide to us to contact you if necessary.

    Security

    All interactions on this website are encrypted with SSL, and we guarantee that we have taken all reasonable measures to ensure the security of any private information you may choose to share with us through this website.

    Consent
